SAT FAQ

Questions before starting.

How often should students meet?

Most students should meet once per week, with homework between sessions. Students close to a test date may benefit from more frequent sessions.

Do students get homework?

Yes. Homework is central to the program and is assigned through the Ascend App whenever possible.

Can you help high scorers?

Yes. High scorers often need pacing, mistake pattern analysis, and advanced strategy rather than basic content review.

Is this better than just using Khan Academy?

Free resources are useful, but many students need structure, accountability, targeted review, and a mentor who can identify what to prioritize.
