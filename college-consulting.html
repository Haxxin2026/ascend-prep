Admissions FAQ

Common parent questions.

Do you write essays for students?

No. The student's voice must remain their own. Coaching focuses on brainstorming, structure, revision, clarity, and strategy.

Can you guarantee admission to a top college?

No. No ethical counselor can guarantee admission. The goal is to help students submit stronger, clearer, more strategic applications.

Can you help younger students?

Yes. Sophomores and juniors can benefit from extracurricular strategy, testing timelines, and long-term application planning.

Can this be combined with SAT tutoring?

Yes. Many students benefit from combining SAT performance coaching with admissions strategy, especially during junior year.
